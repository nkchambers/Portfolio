About Me

Hi there, my name is Nick and I am 30 years old. Hailing from Lake Tapps, WA, I attended Auburn Riverside High School and went on the University of Washington to earn my B.A. in Sociology. Growing up the pacific northwest I've always had a love for the outdoors, whether it's hiking or playing tennis during the summer months or going snowboarding during the winter. One of my favorite things to do rain or shine is going camping, both car and tent, although I have to say backpacking is my all-time favorite.
